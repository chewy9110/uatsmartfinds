About SmartFinds

SmartFinds was started because we believe in sustainability and a circle environment.

In our current world, where climate change is affecting every aspect of our lives,
SmartFinds is born where pre-loved items can find new life by another owner.

SmartFinds seeks to be everyone's platform where we can find good items,
from antique furniture to up-market watches.

We can post our items for free and find someone who will continue to
love them and put them to good use.

Do you have an unused item lying around at home?

Share it on this platform.

Your item may well turn out to be a treasure for someone else.


Our Mission

To contribute to a sustainable world by cultivating circular lifestyle.

To provide a platform to enable anyone to buy and sell online anytime, anywhere around the world.

To be the platform of choice where any pre-loved item is recycled and treasured by a new owner.


Our Vision

To enable everyone to contribute to the circle environment.

To make it easy for anyone to buy and sell online anytime, anywhere around the world.

We envision a world where there will be no waste of unused items, not put to good use.
